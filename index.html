Details of the spot Events

Where Fun Meets Spontaneity – Dive Into Quick Challenges and Big Smiles!

Sudoku

It is a classic numbers-based puzzle event where participants race against the clock to solve grids of increasing difficulty. It sharpens logic, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills, making it an exciting mental challenge for enthusiasts of all levels.

Tic-Tac-Tao (By fliping the cups)

In Tic-Tac-Tao, participants engage in a competitive cup-flipping game, aiming to align their cups in a row to win.

UNO

It is a lively and engaging card game event where participants compete to be the first to get rid of all their cards while strategizing, bluffing, and having fun.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of these events?

Our events aim to encourage creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills through fun and engaging activities. Participants can explore their interests and meet like-minded individuals.

Who can participate?

These events are open to all individuals, including students, professionals, and enthusiasts of various skill levels. Check specific event rules or team size restrictions.

Are there any participation fees?

No, there are no participation fees for our events. Everyone can join for free!

Do I need to register in advance?

Yes, registration is required to ensure a smooth event experience. Please visit our registration page for details.

Will there be prizes for all participants?

The prizes are awarded to winners, all participants will receive recognition for their efforts, and some events may include participation awards.
